Struggles, Patience, and Success.

Matthew Mark Stolarik (born April 12, 1989), is an American-Canadian musician, best known as the drummer and co-lyricist for the metal band Inherent. He is known for his technical proficiency, business attitude, and stamina.

Stolarik grew up in Ottawa, Ontario. During adolescence, he played for at least a dozen bands in pursuit of a career as a full-time drummer. After five long years with the wrong band, he decided to branch out with a new band with hand selected members in 2013. As of 2017, his band Inherent has played with some of the biggest bands in metal music (such as Miss May I, Unearth, and Obey the Brave).

Although Matt’s focus is Inherent, he had other bands on the side to help him explore other genre types. He joined a country band and a pop band to get more exposure in the music community. In 2016, Matt and his side bands have been on Live 88.5 (Ottawa’s Alternative Rock Radio Station) and Much Music (Toronto’s Music Video Television Station).

Interactive Media Design Career Change

Music is great hobby, but it isn’t a realistic career in my opinion. It only happens to those who work at it everyday and want to succeed, either a solo act or as a collective. I needed a change, something that would drive me everyday. With Interactive Media Design, it’s more than anything I’ve ever wanted in a program. In my opinion, it’s the most technical but the most rewarding.
